About Us

Five Seeds Farm is a hybrid. We are a city and country farm in Baltimore, MD. We are family-owned and operated. We are a husband, wife and five children.

The farm began in the city in the spring of 2008, in the Belair-Edison neighborhood in our backyard, a City Farms plot and six vacant lots across the street from our house. We quickly expanded to other vacant lots and private yards across the city. From community gardening, to neighborhood farm stands to community-supported urban agriculture to market and restaurant sales, we proudly grow food for children and community members. We believe in sustainably-grown produce free of synthetic pesticides, herbicides, fungicides and fertilizers. We are a transitional farm seeking organic certification in the 2015 season. We are reviving the idea that farming is essential, artistic and exciting. We champion the idea that true food security is achieved when you control your own food.

In the fall of 2011, Five Seeds Farm made the decision to expand its operation to a much larger tract of land. Though we are still dedicated to the development of city agriculture it was time for us to build a more vibrant business and increase our production potential. Our new farm is in Sparks, MD just west of Interstate 83, 25 miles north of the city. We straddle the fence in sustainable agriculture but continue to build a healthier and vibrant food system. We remain innovators in a progressive agricultural movement. Five Seeds Farm grows fruit, vegetables, herbs, eggs, honey and animals; we actively choose heritage varieties that threaten to disappear from the collective palate. Our focus is to maintain the practice of harmonious diversified agriculture, the cultural and historic legacies of Baltimore's food history and culture, Black farming and homesteading.

Our Mission

We produce beautiful, delicious, chemical-free fruits, vegetables, honey, eggs & meat for the city and engage as community members and children in a sustainable, self-reliant, peaceful, productive lifestyle.
